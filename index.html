CAPABILITIES

Architectural Printing Services

01

Precision Scale Models

Full-color, multi-material architectural scale models from BIM/CAD data. Context models, massing studies, and design development models at 1:50 to 1:500 scale. Client presentations and planning authority submissions.

02

Concrete 3D Printing

Robotic concrete extrusion for structural walls, columns, and custom form elements. Freeform curved geometry that formwork cannot economically produce. Up to 12m × 12m × 8m build volume on-site with mobile rig.

03

Facade Panel Manufacturing

GRC (glass fibre reinforced concrete) and polymer composite facade panels with parametric relief patterns. Unitised cladding systems with any surface texture — no panel mold tooling required.
