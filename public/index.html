Architectural Applications

Residential & Custom Home

Printed concrete foundation walls, bespoke interior feature elements, and custom facade components. Complex curved geometry without formwork cost.

Commercial & Mixed-Use

Cultural & Civic Buildings

What We Offer

Architectural Printing Services

From competition model to poured concrete wall — the full spectrum of architectural additive manufacturing.

🏛️

Precision Scale Models

Full-color, multi-material architectural models at 1:50 to 1:500 scale from BIM/CAD data. Client presentations and planning submissions.

🏗️

Concrete 3D Printing

Robotic concrete extrusion for structural walls, columns, and freeform elements. Up to 12m × 12m × 8m with mobile on-site rig.

🧱

Facade Panel Manufacturing

GRC and polymer composite panels with parametric relief patterns. Unitised cladding systems — no panel mold tooling required.

📐

Parametric Design

Grasshopper/Rhino to print pipeline. Algorithmic surface generation, structural optimisation, and print-ready file preparation.
