Precision Scale Models

Full-color, multi-material architectural scale models from BIM/CAD data. Context models, massing studies, and design development at 1:50 to 1:500.

Concrete 3D Printing

Robotic concrete extrusion for structural walls, columns, and custom form elements. Freeform geometry that formwork cannot economically produce.

Facade Panel Manufacturing

GRC (glass fibre reinforced concrete) and polymer composite facade panels with parametric relief patterns. Any surface texture — no mold tooling required.

End-to-end computational design pipeline from Grasshopper/Rhino to fabrication-ready output. Structural optimisation and G-code generation included.
